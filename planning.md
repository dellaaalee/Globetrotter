What location are you building this guide for, and why did you choose it?
I chose Korea as my destination because I was born and raised there. The place is sentimental to me because I lived my beginning of live there.

Who is your primary audience? (e.g., first-time visitors, backpackers, families, local residents)
My primary audience is for people who are planning their travel to Korea and want to build their itinerary, get recommendations of places to go, things to eat


What do you want visitors to feel or know after spending 5 minutes on your site?
After being on my website for 5 minutes, I want the person to feel like they have a better understanding of what to expect when visitng Korea


What's one design decision — color, layout, or tone — that reflects your destination's identity?
I want the tone to vibrant and modern. Seoul has a mix of modern and traditional city. Most people when visiting the city would see the modern vibe, so I decided the style to be modern with the mix of black and red to convey that feeling when they are visiting the website because the website audience is intented for first time vistor to Korea. 


Design intent:
Modern, sharp, vibrant.
I chose a red and black/white color scheme for this project.
Seoul is a modern city with many skyscrappers. The typograph is also modern, I intentionally didn't add any cursive or other cuter font because I want the website to be modern


Flexbox Layout plan:
I want the food guide and top attraction to be horizational no matter what size I am on. I want photo gallery to be resize based on what size of website I'm in.
I want the spacing to be breathable, I don't like when the website tries to squeeze all the information together and make it not digestable. When I was making the protoype in figma, I made all the choices for alignment and sizing of the container, so I will implement the sizing using flexbox in this milestone. 


Breakpoint plan:
I'm sizing mobile (320px), tablet (768px), dektop (1024px).
I chose those three breakpoint because these three breakpoints are the most common form of device that user will use to access my website, the specific pixel was chosed because it was in the project prompt. 
I will implement the different mobil experience by not just shrinking the elements but switching things like changing the sizing like vertical flow for the food card.
